MPS’s isolated DC/DC converters & modules provide engineers with a superior isolation barrier that more easily meets design specifications, reduces magnetic interference, and comes in a smaller, low-profile size with standard packaging that makes application simple and reliable. They are ideal for a variety of products, including medical and industrial automation, inverters, smart meters, and more.
