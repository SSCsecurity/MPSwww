Battery Management System: Monitor & Protection

  • High Accuracy: Integrated precision ADCs accurately monitor battery current, voltage, and temperature
  • Robust Protection: Programmable hardware-protected cell & pack over/under-voltage, temp, and over-current
  • Reduced BOM Size: Integrated ADCs, cell balancing, plug-in detection, and high-side FET drivers with soft start
  • Quick Time-to-Market: Turn-key reference designs with our fuel gauge and system MCU accelerate design cycles

Lithium-based battery packs require accurate, robust battery management solutions (BMS) to guarantee safety and prolong the useable lifespan of the product. MPS offers a variety of BMS solutions to meet the demanding safety and accuracy requirements for 7 to 16 series cell battery packs. MPSs BMS solutions offer precision voltage, current, and temperature monitoring, which can be used in the system to acquire valuable insights into both the real-time battery pack operating performance and the condition of its individual cells. Our products integrate many space-saving circuits such as high-side protection drivers, current sense, cell balancing, and the MCU LDO. In addition, our BMS products can be paired with our fuel gauges to achieve a turn-key battery pack design that optimizes runtime with individual cell state-of-charge (SoC) monitoring.
